About Us

Prime Collision Center LLC

Prime Collision Center LLC is your trusted partner for professional collision repair and body work in Robinson, IL and Crawford County. Our experienced technicians specialize in restoring vehicles to pre-accident condition using industry-leading techniques and high-quality materials.

We understand that being in an accident is stressful. That's why we work directly with all major insurance companies to handle claims efficiently, making the process as smooth as possible for you. From minor fender benders to major collision damage, we deliver quality results backed by our lifetime warranty.

  • ASE-Certified technicians
  • Insurance claims welcome
  • Frame, body, paint & bumper repair
  • Mechanical & collision repairs in one location
  • Lifetime limited workmanship warranty

Our Services

Collision & Body Work Services

Collision Repair

Complete collision repair including frame straightening, panel replacement, and structural repairs. We restore your vehicle to factory specifications.

Auto Body & Paint

Detailing & Finishing
